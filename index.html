The instructions are very very simple, when you click on a hole, the color of the hole you clicked and 2 of its neighbours will be toggled. The toggling sequence of the lamps:

Lamp lighting -> Yellow lamp -> Red lamp -> Lamp turned off

When all the lamps are turned off, the game is completed.
Additionally, you can use the keyboard.
Ctrl+Z -> Undo
R -> Restart
Up Key or W -> Rotate L
(Rotation button is on the left side of the timer)
H -> Help
